You’re thinking in exactly the right direction. What you’re describing is basically a “build-your-own coffee system” — similar to what you’ve been doing with cookies and ice cream, but expanded so the customer can explore all beans while still anchored to the classic 12 drinks. ☕

The key is structure. Coffee has thousands of possibilities, but if you organize it right it becomes easy and fun for the customer.

1. Keep the “Classic 12” Coffee Drinks (Anchor Menu)

These are the drinks almost everyone recognizes.

Drink	Base Formula
Drip Coffee	brewed coffee
Americano	espresso + water
Espresso	pure espresso
Latte	espresso + steamed milk
Cappuccino	espresso + milk + foam
Flat White	espresso + microfoam
Cortado	espresso + equal milk
Mocha	espresso + chocolate + milk
Macchiato	espresso + small milk
Iced Coffee	brewed coffee + ice
Cold Brew	long extraction coffee
Frappé / blended	coffee + milk + ice

Customer picks one of these first.
