To expand your coffee builder app, toppings and add-ins should be treated as a separate layer after syrup. Think of the drink as a stack of components:

Drink → Bean → Roast → Milk → Sweetener → Syrup → Add-ins → Toppings

This keeps the system clean and lets customers customize without getting confused. ☕
